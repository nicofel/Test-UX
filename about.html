WHO AM I?



Hi, I'm Nicolina Feldt!

Currently a UX-design student in Gothenburg.

After working full-time for five years as a car assembler at Volvo, I
decided to study Java development. Halfway through, I realized that
programming wasn't my jam and that UX design sounded way more
exciting and a better fit for me, so I decided to jump on that train
instead. But by studying programming for a year I learned how to see
things from a programmer's point of view. I am currently learning how
to use my curiosity to talk to people, create innovative, human-centered design solutions, and get comfortable while being
uncomfortable.

I've always been interested in technology since it's a fast-moving
industry with endless possibilities and psychology, and combining the
two is very interesting. I am learning to connect with people,
understand their pain points and problems, and then develop a
solution to fix them.
